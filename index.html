Email (Jingxin.Liu AT xjtlu.edu.cn) | Google Scholar | Github | HomePage@XJTLU

I am currently an Associate Professor in the School of AI and Advanced Computing at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Nottingham under the supervision of Prof. Guoping Qiu in 2018. Before that, I earned my M.Sc. degree from the University of Edinburgh in 2013.

My main research interests include Digital Pathology, Computational Pathology, and Image Processing, with a strong intersection in Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
